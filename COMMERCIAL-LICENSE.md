# Commercial License for Engram Context Continuum

## Why a commercial license?

Engram Context Continuum (EngramCC) is open source under the
[GNU Affero General Public License v3.0 (AGPL-3.0)](LICENSE).

Under AGPL-3.0, anyone can use, modify, and distribute EngramCC
**as long as all derivative works are also released under AGPL-3.0**.
This includes software that interacts with EngramCC over a network
(the "SaaS loophole" that AGPL specifically closes).

If AGPL-3.0 works for your use case, you do not need a commercial license.

## When you need a commercial license

You need a commercial license if you want to:

- Embed EngramCC in a proprietary (closed-source) product
- Offer EngramCC as part of a hosted service without open-sourcing your platform
- Distribute a modified version of EngramCC without publishing your changes
- Use EngramCC in any context where AGPL-3.0 obligations are incompatible with your business

## What the commercial license gives you

- Permission to use EngramCC in closed-source and proprietary software
- No obligation to publish your modifications or derivative works
- No AGPL-3.0 network-use (SaaS) disclosure requirement
- Priority support from the maintainers
